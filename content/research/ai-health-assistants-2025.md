This groundbreaking research provides a systematic thematic analysis of the complex challenges facing AI-powered virtual health assistants in modern healthcare systems. The study examines three critical dimensions: ethical considerations including patient privacy and consent, governance frameworks for regulatory compliance, and usability challenges affecting patient engagement and health outcomes.
