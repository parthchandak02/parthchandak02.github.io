This literature review provides a comprehensive analysis of haptic feedback system evolution, from early tactile interfaces to modern multi-modal haptic experiences. The research examines the intersection of haptic technology with user experience design, highlighting how tactile feedback enhances human-computer interaction across various applications including robotics, virtual reality, and assistive technologies.
